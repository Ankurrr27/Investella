Problem & Solution

The Problem

India's stock market is predominantly male, with women representing only a small portion of investors. This is due to low financial literacy, a lack of confidence, societal stereotypes, and poor access to resources, which hinders women's ability to create wealth and achieve financial independence.

The Solution

Investella is a women-centric finance app designed to bridge this gender gap. It connects women to financial policies, mentors, and gamified learning to help them track goals, plan budgets, and build confidence before they begin investing. The app will use AI chatbots and personalized recommendations to make learning fun and accessible.
